MF DOOM (aka Zev Love X, aka King Geedorah, aka King Ghidra, aka Viktor Vaughn, aka Metal Fingers King, aka Dumile aka DOOM, aka Metal Face, and so on) is a british rapper known for his supervillian stage persona that looks similar to Doctor Doom from Marvel comics. His actual name is Daniel Dumile, and he was born in London on January 9, 1971. He started his carrer in a group with his brother called KMD, and did not take on the name MF DOOM until 1997.
